NOTES & CLARIFICATIONS

Frequently asked

Do you sell Rhino software or licenses?+

No. YAFT Designs is a training and consulting business only. For licenses, go directly to McNeel at rhino3d.com.

Is training online or in person?+

Both. In-person sessions run from our Coimbatore base; remote sessions are available for individuals, teams, and institutions anywhere.

Who are the courses for?+

Architecture and design students, working professionals, and studio/contractor teams who need computational design skills.

Can YAFT run a workshop at our institution?+

Yes, we've delivered workshops at IIT Kharagpur and hold ongoing visiting faculty roles at VIT Vellore, CAT Trivandrum and ASADI. Reach out with your dates and group size.
